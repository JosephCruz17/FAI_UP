Understanding Financial Risk

Make informed decisions by recognizing, measuring, and managing risk.

Types of Risk

  • Credit risk: Borrowing beyond ability to repay.
  • Liquidity risk: Running out of cash when needed.
  • Market risk: Investments may lose value.
  • Insurance gaps: Insufficient coverage for emergencies.

Smart Safeguards

  • Build an emergency fund (1+ month of expenses).
  • Track and limit spending by category.
  • Diversify investments to reduce volatility.
  • Get appropriate insurance and review it yearly.
